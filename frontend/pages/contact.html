Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our laboratory is open Monday-Saturday from 7AM to 9PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you offer home sample collection?

Yes! We offer FREE home sample collection service. Book online or call us to schedule your home collection.

How long do test results take?

Most routine tests are completed within 24-48 hours. Specialized tests may take 3-7 days. You'll be notified via SMS and email when results are ready.

How can I access my test results?

You can access your test results online through our Test Results Portal. You'll need your Registration Number and Diagnostic Number.
