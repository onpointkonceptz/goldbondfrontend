ABOUT GOLDBOND LABORATORIES

Committed to excellence in healthcare diagnostics since 2009

Our Story

Goldond Medical Research and Diagnostics was established with the aim and desire to fill the yearnings of qualitative wide range diagnostic demands of accurate, precise, yet affordable and reliable result.

We engage and maintain services of high grade health professionals who are committed to our visions with deep and clear understanding of our objectives of achieving pinpoint diagnostic and research values to the benefit of our patients and clients..

Our Aim

To be a leading centre in diagnostic and medical research, offering qualitative, wide range of investigations, affordable, accessible and prompt in delivery without compromise in putting smiles on the faces of our clients, customers and patients.

Our Objectives

We strive to operate in conducive, lively & receptive environment, giving patients psychological assurance of satisfaction.

We engage the services of high calibre health and research professionals who are ‘A’s’ in their chosen field.

We operate with modern standard equipment, regularly calibrated and maintained to meet globally acceptable standards.

We get updated in application of latest technology in research and diagnosis.

We are primed in quality assessment and control, timed and prompt delivery of service.

We are in cooperation and association with other reputable institutions in form of collaboration.
